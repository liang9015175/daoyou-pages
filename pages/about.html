Our Mission

Democratizing Gaming Knowledge

We believe every Dota2 player deserves access to professional-level analysis and coaching. Daoyou was built to democratize gaming knowledge, helping players of all skill levels understand their gameplay and improve faster.

Born from a passion for Dota2 and AI technology, Daoyou started as a simple match analyzer and evolved into a comprehensive coaching platform. We combine cutting-edge AI with deep game understanding to deliver insights that were once only available to professional players.
